Third Party Manufacturing

Third Party Manufacturing The Indian Pharmaceutical Industry has experienced a remarkable development in recent years and become the leading generic drug manufacturer and supplier. Jamkas Pharma is one of the leading Third-Party Pharma Manufacturing Company in India that offers the wide range of the pharma medicines that available in the dosage forms like tablets, capsules, injections, and pellets. This company has associated with the reputed pharma companies that want to outsource the best quality pharma medicines under their brand names. Our company provides the best Third-party manufacturing Services in India because we have our own cutting-edge pharma production units in Jammu and Kashmir.
